"Looking to take your maps to the next level? MapTiler SDK's free weather layers have got you covered! With just a few lines of code, you can add current weather and five-day forecast layers to your maps, providing your users with detailed and up-to-date weather information.

Key Features:

  1. Access Updated Weather Layers: Gone are the days of relying on multiple sources for weather data. With MapTiler SDK, you can access regularly updated weather layers, available on MapTiler Cloud.
